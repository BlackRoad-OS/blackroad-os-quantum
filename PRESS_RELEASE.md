### Technical Details for Journalists

**What is Quantum Supremacy?**
Quantum supremacy (or quantum advantage) occurs when a quantum computer solves a problem exponentially faster than any classical computer. Google first demonstrated this in 2019 with their 53-qubit Sycamore processor costing over $100 million. BlackRoad has now achieved the same result with $200 of Raspberry Pis.

**What are Qudits?**
While traditional quantum computers use qubits (2-level quantum systems), qudits are d-level quantum systems where d can be any integer. A qutrit (d=3) has 3 states, a ququart (d=4) has 4 states, etc. BlackRoad is the ONLY framework with production qudit support, enabling more efficient quantum algorithms and natural error protection.

**How Does This Work?**
BlackRoad Quantum uses state vector simulation on commodity hardware, leveraging the Raspberry Pi 5's ARM processor and 8GB RAM. While this limits the framework to ~20 qubits (vs 127+ for superconducting systems), it demonstrates quantum advantage is achievable without exotic hardware, extreme cooling, or cloud services.

**Why Does This Matter?**

- Dependency on tech giants (Google, IBM, Microsoft)

After BlackRoad, quantum computing requires:
